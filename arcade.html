Block Party

When you join a BlockParty lobby, you get to choose a song from our library of Royalty Free music, When the game starts, you'll be teleported into the BlockParty arena. Your goal is simple... to be the last dancer dancing on right blocks.

Party Games

Quick Build Contest, Farm Animal Scavenger Hunt, Musical TNT, Wolf Tag, DIY Creeper Heads and Minecraft Swords, Digging for Diamonds and much more, can you be the winner of this TG mini game? Join now and show us your minecraft skills.

Quake

Quake is a free-for-all, first person shooter game. The first player to get 25 kills wins. The only weapon you have is a hoe, known as a HO HO HO, ( not santa claws xD ) . You can shoot one fast-moving beam at a time with an upgradable cooldown.

Prop Hunt

In Prop Hunt, the Hiders have to change into various blocks or animals and hide, while the Hunters have to seek hiders and find them all.

TNT Run

TNT Run is about, as the name says, running over TNT. While you are running, the blocks you run on disappear with a brief delay. There are multiple floors to run on; the lowest floor being the last floor above the void. The goal is to be the last player alive. seems like easy? try it now then at play.tgnetwork.in.

Tnt Tag

TNT Tag is a minigame in the TNT Games lobby released with Mini games in valocraft. It is similar to, in which players are given TNT and must pass it to another player before it explodes.
